Acha Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Acha grain(fonio millet)
  • 1 Tin or 1 cup of milk
  • Sugar(add to taste)
  • 2 Calabash or bowl for washing of the Acha(fonio millet)
  • A Wooden turner
  • 3-4 Cups of clean water for the cooking

Mode of Preparation

  1. Measure 1 cup of Acha(fonio millet) into the first bowl, add water to surface level and cover for 3 to 5 minutes
  2. Open and stair with hand.
  3. immediately after staring, pour the staired acha(fonio millet) into the second bowl gradually while shaking the bowl you are pouring from so as to allow the tiny stone particles settle below the acha(fonio millet).
  4. Pour away the sand/stone particles and repeat the same process over and over again until you can't see any stone particle. Note: The process to be repeated is the stairing and pouring into the second bowl and not pouring water into the acha(fonio millet) and covering for 3 to 5 minutes.
  5. Add 3 cups of water to a cooking pot place on fire and allow it to boil.
  6. Pour the Acha(fonio millet) into the pot gradually while stirring with the wooden turner to prevent it from sticking together. Once you notice that the Acha(fonio millet) is becoming thicker and not yet soft, add a cup of water and keep stirring.
  7. Bring down from light when soft and add the cup of milk and sugar to taste.
  8. Finally!!! you can then dish out.
